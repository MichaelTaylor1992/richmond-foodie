MY FAVORITE FOOD TRUCKS

Auntie Ning's

The first Filipino food truck in Richmond. With lumpia, pancit and other amazing Filipino food.

Birmie's Fixins

Great BBQ and all the fixins that go with it

BK's Loaded Kitchen

BK's Loaded Kitchen features great soul food.

Boka Taco Truck

Boca Taco Truck fuses Asian inspiration with Mexican cuisine in a unique combination of great flavors

Fantabulous Chef

Fantbulous Chef is a full-sevice kitchen on wheels, offering breakfast, lunch and dinner cooked to order. Chef Zo also offers catering and can serve as a personal chef.

Grilled Meats & Treats

Grilled Meats and Treats if probably my favorite truck in town. Seafood, cheesesteaks, po boys and more. You have to try the crab cheese tater tots. Now with a physical restaurant too in the old Africanne on Main building.

Jus Cukn

Another favorite from Mobile Soul Sunday.

Kravings

The food truck that serves a fusion of Southern and Contemporary American cuisine

Lady Sharon's Soul Food Kitchen

Lady Sharon, serving soul food to celebrate the nostalgia of Grandma's kitchen

Latin Quarter

One of my new favorite trucks, serving amazing Latin food, specializing in smashed plantain sandwhiches

Southern Spud

The food truck, the baked potato, and all the amazing things you can combine with it to make a great meal

Tasty Cuisine 5 by M&D

Tasty cuisine features a unique combination of soul food, seafood, Carribean and Spanish dishes.

Traditionz Mobile Kitchen

Great BBQ and they'll even bring their smoker with them.
